Top up your Co-op Account

Don't use a credit card?

You can still shop online with us even without a credit card or paypal account. In the near future we will accept bank transfer directly from our checkout. Until then, here are some way to put credit on your account.

Pay Cash on Tuesday

Come by to our shop at 50 John Street on a Tuesday and browse a limited selection of our products. You can pay for your purchases with cash and pay extra to have added to your account.

Pay online with internet banking

Send us some money by internet banking. Use your customer number as a reference please. For faster clearance, email or text us with the amount you've sent and we'll charge your account faster (even before the transfer has arrived on occassion).
